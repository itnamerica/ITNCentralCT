• In June, ITNCentralCT celebrated its 10,000th ride and hosted the Founder and President of ITNAmerica, Katherine Freund, for her second stop of a 60-day cross-country Storybook Tour. The event, held June 17 at the Pond House Café in Elizabeth Park in West Hartford, also invited riders and volunteers and supporters and their families to share stories about how transportation access has changed lives.
