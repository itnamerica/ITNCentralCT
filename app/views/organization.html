Board of Directors

Jennie Dixon, ChFC

Management Consultant, DIXONMILLER Financial Services; Windsor

Jennie worked for more than 30 years in private and academic sector environments serving in various capacities. She is currently in private practice as a management consultant to small businesses. After serving on several non-profit boards, she joined the ITNCentralCT board in 2017.

Don Jensen

Engineer (retired), Pratt & Whitney; Wethersfield

With more than 40 years of engineering experience, Donald worked most of his career as a Design Metallurgy/Materials Data Analyst. Donald has been serving as a volunteer driver since June 2009. In August 2009 he accepted an invitation to join the Board of Directors.

Donald’s community service activities include foster parenting and various leadership positions held at the Wethersfield Evangelical Free Church for the past 40 years.

Joe Kolczynski

Engineer (retired), AAA driving instructor; West Hartford

Joe is a retired engineer. Prior to retirement Joe had an extensive career as a technical sales engineer and manager. Prior to retirement Joe was always an active volunteer in a variety of activities; from providing radio reading and transportation services for the blind community to tutoring mathematics for adults working to acquire a GED.

In retirement Joe has been employed, part time, as a driving instructor and is active as an advocate for people with disabilities.

John Lemega

Attorney (retired); West Hartford

John has been on the ITN board and a driver since 2011. He has twice received the Noah Webster Award, bestowed by the West Hartford Chamber of Commerce because of his commitment to the West Hartford community.

John’s volunteer activities extend beyond ITN: he tutors reading to first and second graders at the Rawson School in Hartford; he is a trained spiritual director; he has worked as a hospice volunteer; and he is an active member of St. Patrick – St. Anthony Church in Hartford. In his spare time, he takes theology courses at Hartford Seminary

Mary Silverberg

Speech/Language Pathologist (retired); West Hartford

Mary is a retired speech and language pathologist with more than 40 years of service in the public schools in both New Jersey and Connecticut. In retirement, Mary volunteers much of her time as an advocate for people with disabilities. Mary is legally blind.

Carl Rodenhizer

Board Chair and Treasurer – Real Estate and Facilities, Columbus House New Haven; Wallingford

Carl Rodenhizer has worked in the non-profit field for over 22 years and is currently the Associate Executive Director of the Hartford-based Mercy Housing and Shelter Corporation. He is responsible for fundraising and development, program operations and facility management, and providing support services to families and individuals who are at risk of homelessness.

Patty Wade

Massage Therapist – Owner, A Touch of Health Massage Therapy West Hartford; Manchester

Patty Wade is a massage therapist who graduated from the Fox Institute of Business Massage Therapy program in 2006. She spent her first year of practice in the graduate clinic before opening A Touch of Health Massage Therapy in West Hartford.

She has spent the last 12 years loving what she does for a living. She is a member of the AMTA Sports Massage Team, AMTA Community Service Team, AMTA Emergency Division Team, Alliance for Holistic Living and most recently she has joined ITN as a board member. She is always looking for new opportunities to serve the community.

Staff

Kevin Farmer

Executive Director

Kevin Farmer enjoyed an extensive career in environmental engineering and project management, and has served nonprofits in several positions including board member and chief executive.

In 2013, he was an Encore!Hartford fellow, engaging in a hands-on, intensive development program through this joint venture initiative of Leadership Greater Hartford and the University of Connecticut. Through that program, he strengthened and expanded major partner relationships for the Farmington River Watershed Association.

Kevin lives in West Hartford with his wife Cheryl. They have two children, both in college.
